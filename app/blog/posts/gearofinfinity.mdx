A game developed by main team of Revolution Dimensions Studios.

The game is set in France, and the protagonist is a girl.
See more on the website: https://gamejolt.com/games/Gear_Of_Infinity/823816

Gear Of Infinity is a 2D Platform Game, Metroidvania based on speed with 10 Chapters and a local multiplayer mode to have fun with your friend.
